---------------------------------------- 
How do I update with my video?:
---------------------------------------- 

1. Save your flv video in the "flv" folder.

2. Open "video.xml" in Notepad or Dreamweaver.

3. Update the path to your new video file.

Example: flv/name_of_your_video.flv 

---------------------------------------- 
How do I change the buffer time?:
---------------------------------------- 

1. Open "video.xml" in Notepad or Dreamweaver.

2. Go to the attribute buffTime = "2".

3. Change the value 2 to whatever you want. 2 equals the amount of seconds of playback to preload before the video starts. For larger file sizes you will want to increase the number.

--------------------------------------------------------
How do I turn looping off so the video plays only once?
-------------------------------------------------------- 

1. Open "video.xml" in Notepad or Dreamweaver.

2. Go to the attribute loop = "true".

3. Set it to false for no looping, and true for looping.

--------------------------------------------------------
How do I add my logo?
-------------------------------------------------------- 

1. First save your logo in the "images"folder. It can be a jpg, png, gif or animated swf.

2. Open "video.xml" in Notepad or Dreamweaver.

3. Go to the attribute logo = "images/logo.png" and change the path.

--------------------------------------------------------
How do I turn off/on the logo intro?
-------------------------------------------------------- 

1. Open "video.xml" in Notepad or Dreamweaver.

2. Go to the attribute logoIntro = "true".

3. Set it to false for no logo, and true for logo intro.

--------------------------------------------------------
How do change the url for my enter button?
-------------------------------------------------------- 

1. Open "video.xml" in Notepad or Dreamweaver.

2. Go to the attribute url = "http://activeden.net/user/robwes19" and change the url path

--------------------------------------------------------
How do change the url target window?
-------------------------------------------------------- 

1. Open "video.xml" in Notepad or Dreamweaver.

2. Go to the attribute target = "_self" and change the target to _blank, _parent, _top or _self.

--------------------------------------------------------
How do turn off the fullscreen button?
-------------------------------------------------------- 

1. Open "video.xml" in Notepad or Dreamweaver.

2. Go to the attribute maxScreen = "true" and change true to false.

--------------------------------------------------------
How do change the fullscreen button color?
-------------------------------------------------------- 

1. Open "video.xml" in Notepad or Dreamweaver.

2. Go to the attribute btnColor = "0x663399" and change 663399 to your new hecidecimal color value.
