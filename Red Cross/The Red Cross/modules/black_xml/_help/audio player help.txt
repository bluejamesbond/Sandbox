track.xml

-----------------------------------------
How do I change the music track?
-----------------------------------------
Save your mp3 track inside the "track folder. Open "track.xml" and update the path.

Example:

<trackData>

<track 
       loop = "true"
       btnColor = "0xffff00"
       >track/solidbeats_clear_blue_skies.mp3</track>

</trackData>

-----------------------------------------
How do I turn looping on or off?
-----------------------------------------
Change the attribute loop in the xml to true or false.

Example:

       loop = "true"

-----------------------------------------
How do I change the color?
-----------------------------------------
Change the attribute btnColor in the xml to your new hexidecimal color value.

Example:

       btnColor = "0x00CCff"
