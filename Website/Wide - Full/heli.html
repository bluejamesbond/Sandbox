Guest Speaker At Brown University


Lectured at Brown University to professors and graduate students about Java's painting library and its effiency compared the the Racket library including its Graphics2D, Components, AWT, as well as the constant issue of repaint(). Furthermore, developed a sense of urgency in Racket's development and its essential place in the coming generation.

Topics covered:

  • Racket's state based engine
  • Java vs Racket paint methods
  • Swing and AWT libraries
  • Effieciency and FPS for rendering scenes
  • Level of abstraction/generalization for beginners
